In a mysterious forest, a hidden door appears before you, offering a choice between two paths: one glowing with golden light, the other shrouded in darkness.

Each step forward alters the world around you, revealing secrets or dangers based on your decisions.

As you step onto the glowing path, the air fills with warmth and the trees shimmer with golden leaves. Each step reveals enchanted creatures who offer guidance and rewards, but deeper into the forest, you'll need to solve puzzles to unlock hidden treasures that can aid your journey.

Entering the shadowy path, a chill runs down your spine as the trees twist into haunting shapes. Mysterious whispers follow your every move, and the way forward is treacherous, filled with lurking dangers. However, hidden within the darkness are powerful artifacts, but only for those brave enough to face the challenges ahead.
